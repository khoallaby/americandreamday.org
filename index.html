Why
?

An overwhelming three out of four American adults believe it is harder to get ahead today than it was in the past, and less than one-fourth expect that their children will have more opportunities to advance than they have (source: Allstate/Atlantic Media).

This is a major challenge that we must rise to as a nation. Unfortunately, at the same time that many have felt the American Dream dimming, our sense of common purpose as a nation has waned as well. Divisiveness and polarization are at an all-time high.

This tearing of our social fabric and the distrust it fosters is one of the greatest threats we face. This division holds us back from coming up with win/win solutions that move us forward as a Nation, restore the middle class, and keep the American Dream strong for generations to come.

Declare.

Let’s declare July 3rd as
National American Dream Day.

Let’s recognize all that brings us together as a Nation and come together to bring the American Dream back within reach!
